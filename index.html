Product Methodology
AI产品落地三原则
Three Principles of AI Product Launch
  • 先锚定 “业务痛点” 再匹配 AI 能力（不做 “技术炫技”）
  • 用 “最小可用产品（MVP）” 验证价值（降低试错成本）
  • 数据驱动迭代（核心指标定义→跟踪→优化）
  • Anchor "business pain points" before matching AI capabilities (no "tech showoff")
  • Validate value with MVP (reduce trial-and-error cost)
  • Data-driven iteration (core metric definition → tracking → optimization)

Requirement Trade-off

Cut the "auto-reply" feature in the early stage —— because it relies on "precise intent recognition", while the MVP stage prioritizes the core value of "structuring" to avoid feature overload

Review & Insights

复盘：初期高估了用户对‘自动化’的接受度，后期增加‘半自动化开关’，用户使用率提升40%——收获：产品要‘适配用户习惯’而非‘强行教育用户’

Review: Initially overestimated users' acceptance of 'automation', later added 'semi-automation switch', user usage increased by 40% — Insight: Products should 'adapt to user habits' rather than 'force education on users'
